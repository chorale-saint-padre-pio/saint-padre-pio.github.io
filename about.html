Our Patron

Saint Padre Pio
Our Inspiration

Padre Pio of Pietrelcina (1887 to 1968) was an Italian friar, priest, and mystic. Known for his deep spirituality, compassion, and gifts of healing, he was canonized by Pope John Paul II in 2002. Our choir draws daily inspiration from his example of prayer, humility, and joyful service.
